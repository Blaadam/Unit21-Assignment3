About Tiger Games

Tiger Games was formed in 2018 by Andy Johnson and has grown into a small/medium games development business.  The company now employs around 50 members of staff, and it has an exciting product pipeline of video games expected to release in a few years’ time.  Currently, the company has two live games and a beta released game.  The two games are Warfield and League of Heroes’ and project x which is currently being beta tested by a designated user group.
